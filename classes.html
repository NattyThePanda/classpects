Classes!!
Heir Seer Knight Witch
Heirs are very diverse, friendly but easily influenced by others.
Usually the Aspect is the one who dominates their personality, which is unique to the Heir class.		 Seers love understanding matters that actually matters to them, they are very wary of what's wrong and what's right.
Seers are also very frindly, but really serious when the moment calls for them.		 Knights wear armor, which is self-explanatory, Knight players usually know very little about themselves and wear masks to hide their true selves. Witch players are the friendliest of all the classes, but they are very manipulative and ruthless in their worst.
Maid Page Rogue Thief
Maids act like caretakers, they love doing their best to their best companions, and they work hard to bring a better future. Pages start useless and are very hard on themselves, often in need of others to make them strong. Pages are usually very wise, friendly and good mannered. Rogue players are the ones who needs others the most, they are the most generous players of all the classes, caring first about others. Thief players are the most self-centered of all the classes, very proud of themselves and very selfish, but also very good partners and workers.
Prince Bard Mage Sylph
Princes have a moral that everything must be perfect in their own vision, if they don't, they will destroy it to make what they want a reality.
And just like the real world princes, nothing is mundane to them.		 Bard players are instruments of greatness, but also of destruction.
Usually they are keys to open either great or bad things.
Bards also love to talk about themselves, but also about others.		 Mage players are the most helpful of the classes, what drives them is the feeling or progress. They do right for right's sake, but usually are very hard on themselves. Sylph players are, in essence, healers, they love taking care of others' problems, not only physical but emotional aswell.
The bad thing about them is that they always want to keep track of one's life.
